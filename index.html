About Us

We are a typical family who loves our puppies! Macey, a mini goldendoodle, was thoughtfully chosen at a time when we needed some happiness brought into our lives. A dog that didn’t shed, was perfect. A dog that wasn’t too big, was perfect. A dog that was good with kids, was perfect. A dog that was smart and easy to train, was perfect. Macey was perfect for our family and quickly became a valued member of our household. With the addition of Ashur, and most recently, Millie (a mini Aussiedoodle), our family of fur babies has grown. Now, we just want to share the wonderful gem of a breed that we have discovered- Doodles. Because Doodles are Dandy!

Our Doodles

❮ ❯

The dame is Macey. “Macey” is derived from the medieval male form of Matthew. We adopted Macey right after the loss of a very special “Matthew” in our lives. She was born in September 2017. She is a chocolate, F1b mini goldendoodle and weighs 30 pounds. Her disposition is very calm, sweet and affectionate. She loves on anyone and everyone. A guard dog, she is NOT!

❮ ❯

The sire is Ashur. “Ashur” means “happiness”. We adopted Ashur in order to give Macey a companion and to hopefully have puppies one day. He was born in September 2018. He is a black, CKC registered, miniature poodle that weighs 8 pounds. His personality is somewhat protective, though no one takes his 8 little pounds very seriously. He also loves to snuggle and perch on the top of the couch, like a parrot.
